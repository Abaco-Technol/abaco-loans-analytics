## Calculation and governance
- **Definition:** TPV generated by clients with at least two funded invoices in the last 8 weeks (weekly) or 3 months (monthly).
- **Filters:** Exclude one-off invoices, clawbacks, or charge-offs; enforce debtor and obligor limits; drop invoices with missing debtor tax ID or KYC flags.
- **Data quality:** Require completeness >95% on invoice amount, debtor, due date, and collection status; block release when quality falls below 90%.

## Operating cadence
- **Dashboards:** Daily refresh for ops; weekly executive pack with NSM trend, cohort views, and variance to plan.
- **Actions:** Trigger playbooks for TPV drops by channel, debtor concentration breaches, or DPD/NPL drift; align product/credit with guardrails.
